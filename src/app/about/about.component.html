Electric Vehicle Charging Stations

One of the biggest barriers to electric vehicle uptake is a lack of charging infrastructure. This application brings the ease of charging your electric vehicles by a touch

Out of all the conflicts of running out of fuels and the fluctuations of the prices of fuels electric vehicles came to picture like a Superman saving the world.

An electric charging vehicle station also called ev charging station,is an element in an infrastructure that supplies electric energy for recharging of plug in electric vehicles including electric cars, neighborhood electric vehicles and plug in hybrids Charging an electric car is a simple process where you simply plug your car into a charger that is connected to the electric grid.

These chargers typically fall under one of 3 categories Level 1 charging stations Level 2 charging and DC fast chargers also known as Level 3 charging stations.

So here we are stretching down the starry sky for you by this application where you can book your slots for charging your electric cars at your available time leisurely.
